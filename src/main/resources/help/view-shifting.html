Shifting

The world can be moved by means of WSAD keys or arrows. By default, it is the camera that is moved over the world (W = the User flies northwards). To move the world (W = the world is moved northwards), use Ctrl+M.

By default, at the beginning of a human player's move, the world is moved so that the center of the world be in the center of the world panel. To disable this, use Ctrl+C.
